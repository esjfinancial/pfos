FAQ

Common questions

How does billing work?
All billing is handled through Stripe, the same encrypted payment infrastructure used by millions of businesses. Every account starts with a 14-day free trial (no card required); when you subscribe, your monthly or annual plan begins immediately and renews on the same date. You can change plans, switch to annual, or cancel anytime from your portal — no contracts and no cancellation fees. We'll email you a receipt for every charge.
Is there a free trial?
Yes — every new account starts with a 14-day free trial of Core, with no credit card required. During the trial you can enter your finances, use the everyday calculators, and watch your plan take shape. When you're ready, subscribe to Core ($19/mo) or Planning ($39/mo) — or add your partner with a Couple plan. Bank connections and the AI Planner unlock once you're on a paid plan.
How does Plaid bank-account integration work? CORE
Core and Planning accounts can connect their real bank, brokerage, and credit card accounts through Plaid — the industry-standard secure data bridge trusted by thousands of financial apps. Plaid uses bank-level 256-bit encryption, never shares your credentials with us, and gives you read-only access (we can see balances and transactions, never move money). Once connected, transactions auto-categorize into your spending dashboard and balances update in your net worth view. You can disconnect any account anytime from your portal — your historical data stays, but live syncing stops immediately.
What calculators are included?
PFOS includes 62 financial calculators across 13 categories: Quick Tools, Free Assessments, Loans & Housing, Student Loans (5 tools), Investing & Fees, Insurance & Protection, Infinite Banking (IBC), Retirement Planning, Tax Strategy, Estate & Legacy, Life Events, Behavioral Finance, and Specialty Situations. Every calculator includes visual results, advisor-ready recommendations, and compliance disclaimers where applicable. Calculator availability varies by tier — Core includes the everyday tools (tracking, affordability, protection, the debt-payoff optimizer, buy-vs-rent, and basic estate & beneficiary tracking), while Planning unlocks the retirement-strategy, insurance, tax, college, and estate-planning calculators plus the AI Planner and AI policy auditor. PFOS also includes a built-in library of 100+ plain-English financial explainers, so any term or concept is one click away.
Do I need a financial advisor to use PFOS?
No. PFOS works independently with AI-powered recommendations, 62 calculators, health scores, and the full planning suite — no advisor needed. If you ever want professional guidance, you can request an advisor consultation directly from within the platform. Many users start solo and add an advisor later when they're ready.
Can I change or cancel my plan?
Yes — anytime, with no penalties. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your monthly subscription from your portal. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period (you keep access until then). After that your account goes read-only — your data stays safe, and you can resubscribe anytime to pick up right where you left off.
Is my financial data secure?
Yes. All data is stored in Supabase with row-level security enabled — meaning each user can only access their own data. Connections are encrypted via HTTPS, payments are processed by Stripe (PCI-DSS Level 1 certified), and bank-account integrations use Plaid's read-only encrypted bridge. We never sell your data and it is never used for advertising. You can request a full export or deletion at any time from your portal.
How does the AI recommendation feature work?
PFOS analyzes your full financial profile and generates a complete, personalized plan — several side-by-side approaches, each with specific, prioritized recommendations — so you can compare strategies and act with confidence. Your data is sent to Claude AI without your identity attached, and it's never used for advertising. On Planning, your first full plan is included, and you can refresh it with a re-audit each quarter as your situation changes. When an advisor is involved, every recommendation is saved as a draft the advisor reviews before it reaches you — clients never see AI content that hasn't been reviewed.
What is the difference between an advisory client and a product client?
Advisory clients are linked to a specific financial advisor who manages their profile, generates reports, and provides recommendations. Product clients use PFOS independently without an assigned advisor — they have access to all planning tools but manage their own data. Product clients can request an advisor consultation from within the platform at any time.
Can couples use PFOS together?
Yes. PFOS supports Joint, Separate, and Hybrid household modes for linked couples. Joint households share a single unified financial profile. Separate households maintain independent data but advisors can view both. Hybrid mode keeps each partner's finances separate while sharing specific goals and assets — with ownership splits (say 60/40 on a shared account) that the engine weights correctly for each of you.
Can I give feedback or request features?
Absolutely — and we actively want you to. Click the blue feedback button in the bottom-right corner of any page to submit feedback, report a bug, or request a feature. We review every submission and many directly influence what we build next.
How do I register as an advisor?
Click "Get Started" and register with your email. Advisor accounts go through a brief approval step — typically approved within 1 business day. Once approved you'll have full access to the advisor dashboard and can start adding clients immediately.

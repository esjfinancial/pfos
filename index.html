FAQ

Common questions

What happens when the soft launch ends?
You will receive an email at least 30 days before any pricing activates. At that point you can choose to continue on your current plan, switch to a different tier, or cancel entirely. Nothing changes without your explicit awareness and agreement. We will never charge you without notice.
Do I need a financial advisor to use PFOS?
No. You can use PFOS completely independently as a product client. You'll have access to the full planning tool, financial scores, projections, and all modules. If you want to connect with an advisor, you can request one directly from within the platform.
Is my financial data secure?
Yes. All data is stored in Supabase with row-level security enabled — meaning each user can only access their own data. Connections are encrypted via HTTPS. We never sell your data and it is never used for advertising. You can request a full export or deletion at any time from your portal.
How does the AI recommendation feature work?
Advisors can trigger an AI analysis of a client's full financial profile. The system sends your data (not your identity) to Claude AI which returns specific, prioritized recommendations. These are saved as drafts — the advisor reviews every one before it becomes active. Clients never see AI-generated content that hasn't been reviewed by their advisor.
What is the difference between an advisory client and a product client?
Advisory clients are linked to a specific financial advisor who manages their profile, generates reports, and provides recommendations. Product clients use PFOS independently without an assigned advisor — they have access to all planning tools but manage their own data. Product clients can request an advisor consultation from within the platform at any time.
Can couples use PFOS together?
Yes. PFOS supports Joint, Separate, and Hybrid household modes for linked couples. Joint households share a single unified financial profile. Separate households maintain independent data but advisors can view both. Hybrid mode allows shared goals and tracking while keeping individual data separate.
Can I give feedback or request features?
Absolutely — and we actively want you to. Click the blue feedback button in the bottom-right corner of any page to submit feedback, report a bug, or request a feature. During the soft launch we review every submission and many of them directly influence what we build next.
How do I register as an advisor?
Click "Get Started" and register with your email. Advisor accounts go through a brief approval step — typically approved within 1 business day. Once approved you'll have full access to the advisor dashboard and can start adding clients immediately.
